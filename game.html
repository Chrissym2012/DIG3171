My Digital Narrative

Abstract

A Mother’s Sacrifice is an interactive narrative created in Bitsy where players step into the role of a mother determined to protect her child at all costs. Set in a minimalist pixel world, the game invites players to explore environments, uncover story fragments, and make choices that reveal the emotional and moral weight of parental sacrifice.

By inhabiting the mother’s perspective, players experience firsthand the challenges, fears, and selfless decisions involved in safeguarding a loved one. The simple aesthetic of Bitsy emphasizes intimacy and emotion over complex mechanics, allowing the narrative to remain at the center. A Mother’s Sacrifice aims to immerse players in a poignant, reflective journey, showing how even small interactions can convey deep themes of love, protection, and personal cost.
